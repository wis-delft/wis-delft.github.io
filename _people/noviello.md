## About

Yuri Noviello is a PhD candidate in the Web Information Systems group at TU Delft, supervised by Dr. Gosia Migut. He received his BSc in Computer Science and Engineering and his MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the University of Bologna. Prior to his PhD, he spent one year as a researcher on Computational Linguistics at the University of Bologna.
 
As part of the [AI4SE](https://se.ewi.tudelft.nl/ai4se/index.html) research partnership between TU Delft and [JetBrains](https://www.jetbrains.com), his current research investigates AI applications in computer science education, with a focus on natural language processing techniques for generating and evaluating material that supports student learning.
 
His full list of publications is available on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=dA01N3AAAAAJ).
